Interactive Taxonomic Breakdown of Multiterabase Assemblies

These interactive Sankey Diagrams showcase phylogenetic breakdowns of genome bins produced by GenomeFace on the real multiterabase assemblies featured in its paper. Each diagram displays the taxonomy of bacteria from phylum to genus level for genomes with a quality score of 50 or higher, as determined by CheckM2, with taxonomic assignments inferred by GTDB-Tk.

Labels that are blank (e.g., g__ at genus level or f__ at family level) indicate the genome does not fit any known taxonomic assignment at that level.

Interactive Sankey Diagrams:

  • Twitchell Island Wetlands
  • Harvard Forest
  • Great Redox Experiment

GTDB-Tk and CheckM2 Analysis Raw Outputs

Download the zip file containing the raw GTDB-Tk and CheckM2 analysis outputs for all genome binners used in the paper. GTDB-Tk provides taxonomic assignments from phylum to species level, while CheckM2 produces quality assessments of the genome bins (completeness and contamination scores). All claims and results presented in the paper for the real datasets are based on these outputs.

GenomeFace Bins

These directories contain the FASTA files of genome bins produced by GenomeFace. All genomes may be downloaded using the following command:
